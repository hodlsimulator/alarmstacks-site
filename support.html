FAQs

How do I restore purchases?

Open the app and use Restore Purchases (on the paywall or in Settings). You will not be charged again.

Alarms didn’t sound. What should I check?

  • Ensure the app has permission to schedule alarms/notifications.
  • Low Power Mode or Focus modes can affect timing; AlarmKit alerts should still break through, but double-check device settings.
  • If an alarm was edited very close to its fire time, re-arm it.

How do I turn off Live Activities?

iOS Settings → Notifications → Live Activities.

Deleting my data

Delete the app to remove local data. If you emailed us and want those emails deleted, just ask.
